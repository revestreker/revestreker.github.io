Service Bus

Animated short film

Film

A comedic animated short following a grumpy tour bus driver and his chaotic passengers through the Norwegian countryside. Service Bus premiered at Kortfilmfestivalen Grimstad 2025 and toured the international festival circuit — winning the Norwegian Short Film Award for Best Animation.

Animation production

Animation production for the Fredrikstad Animation Festival 2026 trailer. Lush, hand-painted backgrounds and a full character lineup bring a fantastical world to life — cliffs, mushrooms, and a crew ready to adventure.

The Disappearance of Brita Svahni

Interactive game & animation

Play the game

A hand-animated point-and-click adventure game set in a mysterious Norwegian interior. Brita Svahni has vanished — explore her home, piece together the clues, and uncover what happened. Built with frame-by-frame animation and an original atmospheric soundtrack.

Gangar

Music visuals / Spotify Canvas

Animated Spotify Canvas loops and music visuals for Norwegian folk-jazz act Gangar. Looping, hypnotic hand-drawn character cycles — designed to breathe and move alongside the music.

Ecokrim Concert Visuals

Live VJ work

Animated character cycles and loops performed live through Resolume Arena for Norwegian comedy-crime act Ecokrim. Mike walks through seasons — spring greens, winter greys, and everything in between — synced to the show in real time.

Video files are .mov / large — upload to YouTube or Vimeo as unlisted and send me the links to embed them here.

Among Giants

Animated short film

A hand-drawn animated short about small things in a very large world. Atmospheric, wordless, frame-by-frame — exploring scale, wonder, and the strange tenderness of being tiny.

Teddies / Mini Mic

Animated short series

A cosy murder mystery in miniature. Teddy bears, a tiny jazz club, and a detective who should probably be in a children's show but really isn't. Teddies is the original animation series behind the Mini Mic concept — hand-drawn, character-driven, and gleefully absurd.
