Service Bus

Animated short film

Film

An award winning short film made at The Animation Workshop as our graduation project.

We all know the service button on airplanes, but what about the ones on buses? When a bored traveler stumbles upon one, his curiosity gets the better of him. But with only the bus driver on duty, pressing it might lead to unexpected consequences.

The Disappearance of Brita Svahni

Interactive game & animation

Play the game

Brita Svahni is a well-known theater actress. Nobody has seen her since her last performance. You show up at her apartment to find out what happened. Look around, find clues and piece together the story in this hand-animated point-and-click game with an original atmospheric soundtrack.
